# LUA BIM LABS Personal Tutor Terms and Disclaimers

This document is an operational policy for launch. It is not legal advice. Before scaling paid sales, ask a qualified legal professional to review the final public terms, privacy notice, refund policy, and consumer protection requirements for the target countries.

## Service Name

LUA BIM LABS Personal Tutor

## Service Type

Personalized Telegram-based daily MEP BIM education service with monthly progress assessment, level-based curriculum, and monthly written progress reports.

## Price

USD 119/month

## Payment Method

PayPal Payment Link (USD 119/month subscription).

## What Is Included

- One personalized MEP BIM lesson per day through Telegram, matched to the client's assessed level
- Monthly level diagnostic assessment and result communication
- Monthly written progress report (learning KPIs, level check score, next-month curriculum)
- Weekly BIM Check Friday quiz (every Friday)
- Discipline-specific scenario lessons (5 per discipline per level)
- Corrective micro-lessons targeting identified weak areas
- Limited Q&A eligibility (up to 3 qualifying questions per week via Telegram)
- Level-up criteria tracking and notification when level advancement criteria are met

## What Is Not Included

- Project file review or Revit model QA
- Clash report review or coordination issue resolution
- Engineering design verification or code compliance confirmation
- Construction approval or professional certification
- Legal, engineering, architectural, safety, or professional advice
- Unlimited 1:1 consulting
- Live video or voice sessions
- Emergency support
- Zoom or video call sessions

## Q&A Eligibility Rules

Included Q&A covers lesson-related conceptual questions and study-context clarification questions.

Q&A does not cover project-specific file review, error diagnosis from client models, code compliance questions, legal questions, or questions that require reviewing confidential project materials.

LUA BIM LABS reserves the right to decline questions that fall outside the scope defined above and to redirect the client to an appropriate higher-tier service.

## Educational Disclaimer

All content is provided for general education and training purposes only.

The service does not replace licensed engineering, architectural, construction, legal, or safety advice. Clients must verify all project-specific decisions with qualified professionals, project standards, local laws, codes, and contract requirements.

LUA BIM LABS does not guarantee project approval, employment, certification, construction acceptance, code compliance, or specific business results from completing the Personal Tutor program.

Level assessments and progress reports reflect the client's self-reported and observed performance within this educational service only. They do not constitute professional qualifications or certifications.

## Client Responsibility

The client is responsible for:

- completing the intake form accurately and providing honest self-assessment
- maintaining access to Telegram throughout the subscription period
- applying lessons appropriately to their own project context
- verifying all technical decisions with qualified professionals before project use
- following local laws, standards, contracts, and project requirements
- not treating educational content as final project instruction or professional advice
- not sharing confidential or restricted project material through Telegram

## Confidential Information Policy

Clients must not send confidential project files, private drawings, contracts, personal data, customer data, proprietary model files, or materials they do not have permission to share.

If a client asks a question referencing a real project, they must anonymize project names, company names, client names, locations, and sensitive details before sending.

## Content Reuse Policy

General, non-confidential questions and learning scenarios may be anonymized and reused by LUA BIM LABS for educational content, knowledge base development, blog posts, Telegram lessons, or training material.

LUA BIM LABS will not reuse identifiable client information without explicit permission.

## Delivery Channel

The service is delivered through Telegram. The client must provide a valid Telegram username and maintain active Telegram access.

If Telegram access is blocked, restricted, or unavailable in the client's region or device environment, delivery may be delayed or interrupted. LUA BIM LABS is not responsible for delivery failures caused by platform restrictions on the client's side.

## Refund Policy

- Full refund is available if no lesson has been delivered and the request is made within 24 hours of payment.
- Once educational delivery has started (Day 1 lesson delivered), refunds are not guaranteed.
- If a refund request is received after delivery begins but within the first 7 days, LUA BIM LABS may review the case and issue a partial refund at its discretion.
- After 7 days of delivery, no refunds are issued for the current billing period.
- PayPal disputes and chargebacks are handled according to PayPal's own processes and user agreement.
- Payment processing fees, currency conversion fees, or PayPal fees may not be recoverable.

## Cancellation Policy

- The client may cancel before the next billing cycle to stop future charges.
- Cancellation stops future billing but does not refund already-delivered service in the current billing period.
- The client retains access to lessons through the end of the paid period after cancellation.
- No prorated refunds are issued for mid-period cancellations.

## Service Pause Policy

- Clients experiencing extended unavailability (travel, illness, etc.) may request a service pause of up to 14 days per 3-month period.
- Pause requests must be made via Telegram before the pause period begins.
- Billing continues during a pause; the pause extends the effective service period by the number of days paused (up to 14 days).

## Level Assessment Disclaimer

Level assessments are educational self-assessment tools designed to personalize curriculum delivery. They do not constitute formal certifications, professional qualifications, or competency verification for employment or project work.

Assessment scores are based on the client's own responses and are not independently verified. LUA BIM LABS is not responsible for decisions made by employers, clients, or project teams based on self-reported assessment results.

## Dispute Prevention Rules

To reduce disputes:

- Show price clearly as USD 119/month.
- State that Personal Tutor is educational content and personalized curriculum only.
- State clearly what is included and excluded (particularly: no project file review, no video sessions).
- Require intake form completion and consent before onboarding.
- Keep payment records and Telegram delivery logs.
- Do not promise outcomes such as employment, certification, project approval, or salary increases.

LUA BIM LABS complies with PayPal's User Agreement and Acceptable Use Policy. PayPal may process disputes, refunds, chargebacks, and claims under its own rules.
