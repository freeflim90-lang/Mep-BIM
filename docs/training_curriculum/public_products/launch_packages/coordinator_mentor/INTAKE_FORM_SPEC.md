# Coordinator Mentor — Intake Form Specification
## LUA BIM LABS | Service: Coordinator Mentor | USD 229/month
**Version:** 1.0 | Effective: 2026-06-01

---

## PURPOSE

This document specifies every field of the Coordinator Mentor intake form. This form is filled by the client before the service begins. It serves three purposes:
1. Confirm identity and payment
2. Provide context for personalized lesson delivery
3. Set service expectations (what is and is not included)

The form is delivered as a Google Form. The link is shared on the service landing page and in direct sales conversations.

## SECTION 1: PERSONAL INFORMATION

**1.1 Full Name**
- Type: Short text (required)
- Label: "Your full name (as you'd like it on your certificate)"
- Validation: Minimum 2 characters

**1.2 Email Address**
- Type: Email (required)
- Label: "Email address"
- Note text: "Use the same email address you'll use for PayPal."

**1.3 Country of Residence**
- Type: Dropdown (required)
- Label: "Country"
- Options: Full list of countries (alphabetical). Default: none selected.

**1.4 Time Zone**
- Type: Dropdown (required)
- Label: "Your time zone"
- Options: UTC-12 through UTC+14 with major city references (e.g., UTC+8 — Singapore/Beijing/KL/Perth)

**1.5 Telegram Username**
- Type: Short text (required)
- Label: "Your Telegram username"
- Helper text: "Example: @yourname. This is how we'll deliver your lessons and communicate with you. Make sure your Telegram privacy settings allow messages from new contacts."
- Validation: Must begin with @ (client-facing note, not automated)

## SECTION 3: COORDINATOR EXPERIENCE

**3.1 How many years have you been working as a MEP BIM Coordinator?**
- Type: Multiple choice (required)
- Options:
  - "Less than 1 year"
  - "1–2 years"
  - "2–4 years"
  - "4–7 years"
  - "More than 7 years"

**3.2 What types of MEP coordination have you been involved in?**
- Type: Checkboxes (select all that apply, required — minimum 1)
- Options:
  - "HVAC vs. Structural clash resolution"
  - "Electrical vs. HVAC routing coordination"
  - "Plumbing vs. Structural coordination"
  - "Fire protection system coordination"
  - "Façade and MEP interface coordination"
  - "Coordination between multiple MEP subcontractors"
  - "Coordination on federated models (combined from multiple disciplines)"
  - "I have not been directly involved in formal coordination yet"

**3.3 Which software tools do you currently use for BIM coordination?**
- Type: Checkboxes (select all that apply, required — minimum 1)
- Options:
  - "Autodesk Navisworks Manage"
  - "Autodesk Navisworks Simulate"
  - "Autodesk Construction Cloud (ACC) / BIM 360"
  - "Revit (for review/viewing, not modeling)"
  - "BIMcollab"
  - "Solibri"
  - "Trimble Connect"
  - "Other"
- If "Other" selected: Show short-text follow-up: "Please specify which tools:"

**3.4 How would you describe your current skill level in MEP coordination?**
- Type: Linear scale (required)
- Scale: 1–5
- Label 1: "Beginner — I follow guidance from others"
- Label 5: "Advanced — I lead coordination independently"

**3.5 What is your biggest coordination challenge right now?**
- Type: Paragraph text (required)
- Label: "Describe the main coordination problem, frustration, or skill gap you're dealing with at the moment."
- Helper text: "Be as specific as you can. For example: 'I struggle to manage clash resolution when multiple subcontractors are pushing back on responsibility.' There are no wrong answers — this helps us make the lessons more relevant to you."
- Minimum: 30 characters
