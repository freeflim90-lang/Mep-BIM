# Project Mentor — Intake Form Specification
## LUA BIM LABS | Service: Project Mentor | USD 490/month (Standard) | USD 790/month (Intensive)
**Version:** 1.0 | Effective: 2026-06-01

---

## PURPOSE

This document specifies every field of the Project Mentor intake form. It is filled by the client before the service begins. It serves four purposes:
1. Confirm identity, package selection, and payment
2. Provide context for a personalized mentoring program from Day 1
3. Set the baseline for the quarterly Career Growth Assessment
4. Set service expectations (what is and is not included)

The form is delivered as a Google Form. The link is shared on the service landing page and in direct sales conversations.

## SECTION 6: CAREER GOALS

**6.1 What is your primary career goal for the next 2–3 years?**
- Type: Multiple choice (required)
- Options:
  - "Deepen my skills and stay in the BIM Lead role — I want to be excellent at what I do"
  - "Move into a BIM Project Manager or BIM Director role"
  - "Move into independent consulting (freelance or own firm)"
  - "Move into a client-side / developer BIM role"
  - "Transition to BIM academia or training"
  - "I'm not sure yet — I want to explore options"

**6.2 What are your specific mentoring goals for the next 3 months?**
- Type: Paragraph text (required)
- Label: "Describe 2–3 specific things you want to be able to do — or do significantly better — in the next 90 days."
- Helper text: "Example: 'I want to be able to write a complete BEP from scratch without needing to ask my manager. I also want to establish a QA/QC checklist system for my team.' The more specific you are, the more targeted your mentoring will be."
- Minimum: 50 characters

**6.3 What is your biggest professional challenge right now?**
- Type: Paragraph text (required)
- Label: "Describe the most difficult aspect of your current role or project — the thing that keeps you up at night or that you feel least confident about."
- Helper text: "There are no wrong answers. This is confidential and helps us focus the program."
- Minimum: 50 characters

**6.4 Where do you think you have the biggest skill gap relative to your career goal?**
- Type: Paragraph text (required)
- Label: "What do you think you most need to develop?"
- Helper text: "For example: 'My technical BIM skills are strong but I struggle with managing subcontractors who push back on coordination.' Or: 'I've never written a BEP from scratch and I feel I need to understand it at a deeper level.'"
- Minimum: 30 characters
