# LUA BIM LABS Coordinator Mentor Terms and Disclaimers

This document is an operational policy for launch. It is not legal advice. Before scaling paid sales, ask a qualified legal professional to review the final public terms, privacy notice, refund policy, and consumer protection requirements for the target countries.

## Service Name

LUA BIM LABS Coordinator Mentor

## Service Type

Advanced MEP BIM mentoring service for coordination-stage practitioners, delivered through Telegram daily mentoring, monthly 30-minute Zoom session, coordination template pack, case studies, and scenario challenges.

## Price

USD 229/month

## Payment Method

PayPal Payment Link (USD 229/month subscription).

## What Is Included

- Daily Telegram-based MEP BIM mentoring (Monday–Friday), tailored to the client's coordination stage
- One 30-minute Zoom session per month (scheduled in advance via Calendly or email)
- Zoom session summary memo delivered via Telegram after each session
- Coordination Template Pack (5 files delivered within 3 days of onboarding):
  - Clash Priority Matrix (Excel)
  - Coordination Meeting Agenda (Word)
  - Issue Log Master (Excel)
  - Clash Group Naming Convention Guide (PDF)
  - Weekly Clash Status Report (Word)
- Access to 2 coordination case studies
- One coordination scenario challenge with structured feedback
- 45-day completion certificate upon completion of the structured curriculum
- Q&A via Telegram (Coordinator-scope questions, up to 5 per week)
- Weekly Clash Status Review (written feedback on client-reported coordination issues)

## What Is Not Included

- Review of original confidential model files, contracts, or client project documents
- Engineering design verification or code compliance confirmation
- Architectural, structural, legal, or professional certification advice
- Construction approval or regulatory authority submission support
- Unlimited Zoom sessions (one session per calendar month only)
- Emergency or urgent outside-hours response
- Issue resolution for which a licensed engineer's judgment is required
- Revit model QA or hands-on modeling corrections

## Zoom Session Policy

Each calendar month includes one 30-minute Zoom session. Sessions must be scheduled at least 48 hours in advance.

If the client does not show for a scheduled session without 24-hour notice, the session is forfeited for that month.

If LUA BIM LABS needs to reschedule, the client will receive an alternative time within 7 days.

Additional sessions beyond the monthly included session are not available under this plan. Clients requiring more frequent video sessions should consider the Project Mentor plan.

## Q&A Eligibility Rules

Included Q&A covers coordination workflow questions, clash strategy, issue log management, meeting facilitation, and template usage.

Q&A does not cover project-specific confidential file review, engineering design decisions, code compliance, legal questions, or questions requiring review of restricted materials.

## Educational and Mentoring Disclaimer

All content and mentoring is provided for general education, professional development, and skill-building purposes.

The service does not replace licensed engineering, architectural, construction, legal, or safety advice. All coordination decisions on live projects must be verified with qualified professionals, applicable standards, local regulations, and contract documents.

LUA BIM LABS does not guarantee project success, clash resolution outcomes, client approval, employment, or any specific business result from completing the Coordinator Mentor program.

The 45-day completion certificate is an educational credential issued by LUA BIM LABS. It is not a professional certification, industry qualification, or government-recognized credential.

## Client Responsibility

The client is responsible for:

- completing the intake form accurately, including coordination experience level and project context
- maintaining access to Telegram and Zoom throughout the subscription period
- scheduling Zoom sessions in advance and attending as scheduled
- applying mentoring guidance appropriately to their own project context
- verifying all technical and coordination decisions with project-qualified professionals
- not sharing confidential project files, drawings, contracts, or client data through Telegram or Zoom
- following local laws, standards, contracts, and project requirements

## Confidential Information Policy

Clients must not share confidential project files, private drawings, proprietary models, contracts, client company data, or any materials restricted by NDA or project confidentiality agreements.

If discussing real project scenarios, the client must anonymize project names, company names, client identities, locations, and all identifying information before sharing.

LUA BIM LABS does not accept liability for any breach of confidentiality caused by the client sharing restricted project information through Telegram or Zoom.

## Template Pack Policy

The Coordination Template Pack is provided for the client's personal professional use. Templates may be adapted for live project use.

Templates may not be resold, redistributed commercially, published, or shared with third parties outside the client's direct project team without written permission from LUA BIM LABS.

## Content Reuse Policy

General, non-confidential learning scenarios and coordination questions may be anonymized and reused by LUA BIM LABS for educational content, training material, blog posts, or case study development.

LUA BIM LABS will not reuse identifiable client information without explicit permission.

## Delivery Channel

Daily mentoring and communications are delivered through Telegram. Monthly sessions are conducted via Zoom. Clients must maintain access to both platforms.

LUA BIM LABS is not responsible for delivery failures caused by platform restrictions, internet connectivity issues, or device access issues on the client's side.

- Once mentoring delivery or Zoom session has begun, refunds are not guaranteed.
- If a refund request is received within the first 7 days and no Zoom session has occurred, LUA BIM LABS may review and issue a partial refund at its discretion.
- After 7 days of delivery, no refunds are issued for the current billing period.
- The Coordination Template Pack, once delivered, is considered a delivered service component and is not refundable.
- PayPal disputes and chargebacks are handled according to PayPal's own processes.

## Cancellation Policy

- The client may cancel before the next billing cycle to stop future charges.
- Cancellation stops future billing but does not refund already-delivered service in the current billing period.
- Remaining Zoom sessions in the current billing period may still be used after cancellation, before the period ends.
- No prorated refunds are issued for mid-period cancellations.
