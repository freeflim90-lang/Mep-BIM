# Personal MEP BIM Tutor Pricing and Payment Plan

## Pricing Position

LUA BIM LABS Personal MEP BIM Tutor should be priced as a personalized mentoring service, not as a simple content subscription.

The price should reflect:

- level diagnosis
- personalized daily lessons
- Telegram Q&A
- weak-point tracking
- Obsidian-based learning records
- project and career guidance

## Launch Strategy

First launch only the Starter plan. Do not open Personal Tutor, Coordinator Mentor, or Project Mentor publicly until the Starter funnel proves demand.

Reason:

- lower delivery risk
- simple PayPal payment link setup
- easy onboarding
- good validation for global demand
- prevents unlimited 1:1 support before service operations are mature

## Starter Service Policy

Starter includes:

- one daily MEP BIM lesson through Telegram
- beginner-friendly explanation
- one practical checklist or action item
- monthly topic path
- public blog-linked learning direction

Starter does not include:

- unlimited 1:1 Q&A
- project file review
- Revit model checking
- clash report review
- personalized career mentoring
- urgent support

Optional launch rule:

- allow one short clarification question per week
- move deeper questions to the Personal Tutor Coming Soon list

## Payment Method Strategy

### Phase 1: Fast Launch

Use PayPal payment links and manual onboarding for both domestic and international clients.

- Primary: PayPal Payment Links for one-month purchase
- Secondary: PayPal Subscription link only after the first launch validates demand
- Onboarding: Google Form or Telegram intake form
- Delivery: Telegram 1:1 chat
- Records: Obsidian client profile and learning notes

### Phase 2: Subscription Operation

Add recurring billing after Starter demand is validated.

- Primary: PayPal Subscription for Starter
- Plan management: Starter only
- Future plans remain Coming Soon
- Cancellation: client can cancel before the next billing cycle
- Upgrade: Starter to Personal Tutor only after that plan officially opens

### Phase 3: Productized Portal

Build a small payment and client dashboard.

- plan selection
- payment status
- Telegram ID registration
- level diagnosis form
- learning progress
- invoice and receipt history

## Payment Provider Notes

### PayPal

PayPal is the default payment provider for global and Korean clients.

Operational notes:

- Payment links are simple and work well with blog, Telegram, email, and social media funnels.
- PayPal Payment Links can be used without a custom checkout website.
- PayPal Subscriptions should be used for recurring monthly plans after the offer is validated.
- Client onboarding should start only after payment confirmation.

### Toss Payments

Exclude Toss Payments from the initial payment flow. Reconsider only if PayPal creates a major conversion problem later.

### Stripe

Keep Stripe as a future secondary global option if PayPal conversion or availability becomes a problem.

## Refund and Cancellation Policy Draft

- Monthly plans renew every month unless cancelled.
- The client may cancel before the next billing date.
- If no personalized lesson has been delivered, full refund is possible.
- After personalized diagnosis or lesson delivery begins, refund is prorated or not available depending on service use.
- Project Mentor refunds follow the signed scope and milestone agreement.
