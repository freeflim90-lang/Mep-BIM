# LUA BIM LABS Project Mentor Terms and Disclaimers

This document is an operational policy for launch. It is not legal advice. Before scaling paid sales, ask a qualified legal professional to review the final public terms, privacy notice, refund policy, and consumer protection requirements for the target countries.

## Service Name

LUA BIM LABS Project Mentor

## Service Packages

| Package | Price | Key Addition |
|---|---|---|
| Standard | USD 490/month | 1-hour monthly Zoom + 24h priority response |
| Intensive | USD 790/month | Standard + 2nd monthly Zoom + weekly issue review |

## Service Type

High-intensity MEP BIM professional mentoring for BIM leads, senior coordinators, and BIM PMs, delivered through Telegram daily mentoring, monthly Zoom strategy session, project document template pack, monthly delivery simulation, and quarterly career growth assessment.

## Payment Method

PayPal Payment Link (Standard USD 490/month; Intensive USD 790/month subscription).

## What Is Included — Standard Package

- Daily Telegram-based MEP BIM mentoring (Monday–Friday), tailored to the client's current project phase and role
- One 1-hour Zoom strategy session per month, within 10 days of onboarding start
- Zoom session summary memo (5–7 lines) delivered via Telegram after each session
- 24-hour priority response to Telegram questions (excluding public holidays and pre-notified absences)
- Project Document Template Pack (7 files delivered within 3 days of onboarding):
  - BEP Template Full (Word, 15 sections, ISO 19650-aligned)
  - QA/QC Checklist HVAC (Excel, 35 items)
  - QA/QC Checklist Piping (Excel)
  - QA/QC Checklist Electrical (Excel, 33 items)
  - Coordination Report Template (Word)
  - Model Submission Checklist (Excel)
  - Team Task Tracker (Excel)
- Monthly delivery simulation with written structured feedback
- Quarterly Career Growth Assessment (self-assessment form + LUA BIM LABS written feedback)
- Q&A via Telegram on coordination, BEP, QA/QC, team management, delivery, and career strategy topics

## What Is Included — Intensive Package

All Standard Package items, plus:

- Second 1-hour Zoom session per month (2 sessions total per calendar month)
- Weekly written review of client-reported project issues (up to 2 issue summaries per week)
- Priority scheduling for Zoom sessions

## What Is Not Included (Both Packages)

- Review of original confidential model files, contracts, or restricted client project documents
- Engineering design verification, structural analysis, or code compliance confirmation
- Architectural, legal, or professional certification advice
- Construction approval or regulatory authority submission support
- Indefinite session extensions beyond scheduled Zoom duration
- Modeling corrections or direct Revit model editing
- Emergency response outside business hours for non-critical questions
- Guarantee of project delivery success, client approval, or regulatory compliance

## Zoom Session Policy

Standard Package includes one 60-minute Zoom strategy session per calendar month. Intensive Package includes two 60-minute sessions per calendar month.

Sessions must be scheduled at least 48 hours in advance.

If the client does not attend a scheduled session without 24-hour notice, the session is forfeited for that month.

If LUA BIM LABS needs to reschedule, an alternative time will be offered within 7 business days.

Sessions exceeding 60 minutes are not guaranteed. If the agenda requires more time, remaining topics will be carried to the next month's session.

## 24-Hour Priority Response Policy

LUA BIM LABS will respond to Standard and Intensive clients' Telegram questions within 24 hours during regular business days.

Exceptions:
- Public holidays (Korea and any pre-notified international holidays)
- Pre-notified LUA BIM LABS absence periods (communicated at least 48 hours in advance via Telegram)
- Force majeure events (platform outages, natural disasters, etc.)

Priority is: Project Mentor clients → Coordinator Mentor → Personal Tutor → Starter.

## Project Issue Review Scope

LUA BIM LABS may review coordination issue summaries, BEP text, report structures, team management scenarios, and delivery planning that the client describes or writes.

LUA BIM LABS will not review original model files, client company confidential documents, signed contracts, proprietary drawings, or any materials the client does not have independent authority to share.

All review feedback is educational guidance, not a professional engineering opinion. Project decisions must be verified with qualified project professionals.

## Delivery Simulation Policy

Each calendar month, LUA BIM LABS sends one virtual delivery scenario to the client. The client prepares a written response within 5–7 days. LUA BIM LABS provides written structured feedback based on four evaluation criteria: Completeness, Quality Standards, Risk Identification, and Execution Feasibility.

Scenarios are fictional and anonymized. No real project files are required.

## Quarterly Career Growth Assessment Policy

Every three months, LUA BIM LABS sends the Career Growth Assessment form. The client completes the self-assessment and returns it within 7 days.

LUA BIM LABS provides a 1-page written Career Growth Report within 10 business days of receiving the completed form.

The Career Growth Report is an educational feedback document, not a professional competency certification or employment reference.

## Template Pack Policy

The Project Document Template Pack is provided for the client's personal professional use. Templates may be adapted for live project use.

Templates may not be resold, redistributed commercially, published, or shared with third parties outside the client's direct project team without written permission from LUA BIM LABS.

ISO 19650-aligned templates are based on publicly available international standards. LUA BIM LABS does not guarantee that templates will satisfy all requirements of any specific project EIR, client specification, or local regulatory authority.

## Educational and Mentoring Disclaimer

All mentoring content, template materials, simulation feedback, and career assessments are provided for professional development and educational purposes.

The service does not replace licensed engineering, architectural, construction, legal, or safety advice. All project decisions on live projects must be verified by qualified professionals, applicable standards, local regulations, and contract documents.

LUA BIM LABS does not guarantee project success, delivery acceptance, client approval, employment, salary increase, certification, or any specific business result from the Project Mentor program.

## Client Responsibility

The client is responsible for:

- completing the intake form accurately, including current project context and scope confirmation
- maintaining Telegram and Zoom access throughout the subscription period
- scheduling Zoom sessions in advance and attending as scheduled
- applying mentoring guidance appropriately to their own project and organizational context
- verifying all technical, delivery, and coordination decisions with qualified project professionals
- not sharing confidential client files, signed contracts, NDA-protected drawings, or restricted project materials through Telegram or Zoom
- following all applicable laws, standards, contracts, and project governance documents

## Confidential Information Policy

Clients must not share original confidential project files, private drawings, proprietary models, contracts, client company data, or any materials restricted by NDA or project confidentiality agreements through Telegram or Zoom.

If discussing real project scenarios, the client must anonymize project names, company names, client identities, locations, and all identifying information before sharing.

LUA BIM LABS does not accept liability for any breach of confidentiality caused by the client sharing restricted project information.

## Content Reuse Policy

General, non-confidential learning scenarios, delivery simulation responses, and career development discussions may be anonymized and reused by LUA BIM LABS for educational content, training material, blog posts, or future curriculum development.

LUA BIM LABS will not reuse identifiable client information without explicit written permission.

## Scope Change Policy

If a client's project scope changes significantly (e.g., new project phase, change in role), the client may discuss scope adjustment with LUA BIM LABS. Changes to the service scope that exceed the current package may require upgrading to the Intensive package or a custom arrangement.

## Refund Policy

- Full refund is available if no mentoring has been delivered and no Zoom session has occurred, requested within 24 hours of payment.
- Once mentoring delivery or Zoom session has begun, refunds are not guaranteed.
- If a refund request is received within the first 7 days and no Zoom session has occurred, LUA BIM LABS may review and issue a partial refund at its discretion.
- After 7 days of delivery, no refunds are issued for the current billing period.
- The Project Document Template Pack, once delivered, is considered a delivered service component and is not refundable.
- PayPal disputes and chargebacks are handled according to PayPal's own processes.

## Cancellation Policy

- The client may cancel before the next billing cycle to stop future charges.
- Cancellation stops future billing but does not refund already-delivered service in the current billing period.
- Remaining Zoom sessions in the current billing period may still be used after cancellation, before the period ends.
- No prorated refunds are issued for mid-period cancellations.

## Dispute Prevention Rules

To reduce disputes:

- Show price clearly as USD 490/month (Standard) or USD 790/month (Intensive).
- State clearly that each package includes a fixed number of Zoom sessions per month (not unlimited).
- State clearly what is included and excluded (particularly: no model file review, no engineering advice, no guarantee of project success).
- Require intake form completion and scope confirmation before onboarding.
- Keep payment records, Telegram delivery logs, Zoom session records, and simulation feedback records.
- Do not promise project success, certification, employment, salary outcomes, or client approval.

## PayPal Compliance Notes

LUA BIM LABS complies with PayPal's User Agreement and Acceptable Use Policy. PayPal may process disputes, refunds, chargebacks, and claims under its own rules.
