# LUA BIM LABS Starter Plan Terms and Disclaimers

This draft is an operational policy for the first launch. It is not legal advice. Before scaling paid sales, ask a qualified legal professional to review the final public terms, privacy notice, refund policy, and consumer protection requirements for the target countries.

## Service Name

LUA BIM LABS Starter Plan

## Service Type

Telegram-based educational content service for MEP BIM beginners and early-stage BIM learners.

## Price

USD 39/month

## Payment Method

PayPal Payment Link.

## What Is Included

- One practical MEP BIM lesson per day through Telegram
- Beginner-friendly explanation
- Basic checklist or action item
- Monthly learning direction
- Limited launch-stage clarification support

## What Is Not Included

- Project file review
- Revit model QA
- Clash report review
- Engineering design verification
- Code compliance confirmation
- Construction approval
- Legal, engineering, architectural, safety, or professional certification advice
- Unlimited 1:1 consulting
- Emergency support

## Educational Disclaimer

All content is provided for general education and training purposes only.

The service does not replace licensed engineering, architectural, construction, legal, or safety advice. Clients must verify all project-specific decisions with qualified professionals, project standards, local laws, codes, and contract requirements.

LUA BIM LABS does not guarantee project approval, employment, certification, construction acceptance, code compliance, or specific business results.

## Client Responsibility

The client is responsible for:

- applying lessons appropriately to their own context
- verifying technical decisions before project use
- following local laws, standards, contracts, and project requirements
- not treating general educational content as final project instruction
- not sharing confidential or restricted project material through Telegram

## Confidential Information Policy

Clients must not send confidential project files, private drawings, contracts, personal data, customer data, proprietary model files, or materials they do not have permission to share.

If a client asks a question, they should anonymize project names, company names, locations, and sensitive details.

## Content Reuse Policy

General, non-confidential questions may be anonymized and reused by LUA BIM LABS for educational content, knowledge base development, blog posts, Telegram lessons, or training material.

LUA BIM LABS should not reuse identifiable client information without permission.

## Delivery Channel

The service is delivered through Telegram. The client must provide a valid Telegram username and keep Telegram accessible.

If Telegram access is blocked, restricted, or unavailable in the client's region or device environment, delivery may be delayed or impossible.

## Refund Policy

Suggested launch refund policy:

- Full refund is available if no Telegram lesson has been delivered.
- Once educational delivery has started, refunds are not guaranteed.
- If a refund is requested after delivery begins, LUA BIM LABS may review the case and decide whether a partial refund is appropriate.
- PayPal disputes and chargebacks are handled according to PayPal's own processes and user agreement.
- Payment processing fees, currency conversion fees, or PayPal fees may not be recoverable depending on PayPal policy.

## Cancellation Policy

For one-month payment links:

- Access covers the purchased month.
- The client can choose not to renew.

For future PayPal subscriptions:

- The client may cancel before the next billing cycle.
- Cancellation stops future billing but does not automatically refund already delivered service.
