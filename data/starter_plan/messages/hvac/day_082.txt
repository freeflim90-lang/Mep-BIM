Day 82 - HVAC and Structural Coordination — Penetrations and Hangers

1. Why This Matters
HVAC ductwork is often the largest element competing for space with structure. Coordinating penetrations and hanger points before concrete is poured is far less expensive than coordinating after.

2. Core Concept
- Slab penetrations: where ductwork passes through a concrete slab, a sleeve or opening must be coordinated and submitted before the slab is poured
- Submit a penetration schedule: grid reference, elevation, size, fire rating, and discipline for each slab opening
- Beam web penetrations: duct passing through a structural steel beam web requires structural engineer approval — location, size, and reinforcing details
- Hanger spacing: typically every 2400mm for rectangular ducts under 600mm wide; hanger attachment points must not conflict with structural elements
- Combined loading: when HVAC, plumbing, and electrical hang from the same structural bay, check cumulative point loads do not exceed slab capacity

3. Real Project Lens
On a hospital project, 47 slab penetration requests were submitted. The structural engineer approved 39, requested repositioning for 6 (too close to column heads), and rejected 2 (penetrations through post-tensioned slab ribs). Reviewing all 47 in the BIM model before drilling began saved an estimated 3 days of post-construction remediation work.

4. BIM Check
- Check: every duct crossing a structural slab is submitted as a penetration request with grid reference, elevation, and size
- Check: duct hanger attachment points do not coincide with structural beams or slab ribs
- Check: beam web penetrations have received structural engineer approval before the duct route is finalized

5. Common Mistake
Beginners model the duct route without considering where physical hangers will attach. In dense ceiling voids, hanger locations become a coordination issue in their own right.

6. Today's Action
For one duct run in a model or sketch, identify two points where trapeze hangers would be required. Note whether each attachment point is clear of the structure.
