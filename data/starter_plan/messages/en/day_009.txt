Day 9 - Sprinkler Coordination Is More Than Head Layout

1. Why This Matters
Sprinkler modeling affects ceiling devices, branch pipes, structural coordination, and maintenance access. It also interacts heavily with architectural ceiling plans.

2. Core Concept
A sprinkler layout should be reviewed with ceiling grids, lights, diffusers, detectors, beams, branch pipe routes, and access requirements in mind.

3. Real Project Lens
A sprinkler head may be positioned correctly in plan but still conflict with a ceiling device, beam zone, or branch pipe routing constraint.

4. BIM Check
- Compare sprinkler heads with ceiling device layout.
- Review branch pipe route and support space.
- Check coordination with beams and ceiling zones.

5. Common Mistake
Beginners often place heads first and think the system is complete before reviewing branch pipe routing.

6. Today's Action
Take a small ceiling area and list every ceiling device that must coordinate with sprinkler heads.

Scope note:
This is general educational content, not project review, engineering verification, code compliance confirmation, or construction approval.
