Day 19 - Worksets and Model Sharing Discipline

1. Why This Matters
Worksets affect visibility, ownership, performance, and collaboration. Poor workset habits can slow down the whole BIM team.

2. Core Concept
A workset is a collaboration container in a Revit workshared model. It helps control who works on what and how linked or internal elements are organized.

3. Real Project Lens
If MEP elements are placed on the wrong workset, they may disappear in views, export incorrectly, or confuse coordination filters.

4. BIM Check
- Check the active workset before modeling.
- Review whether linked models and MEP elements are on expected worksets.
- Use worksets carefully with view templates and exports.

5. Common Mistake
Beginners often ignore the active workset and create cleanup work for others.

6. Today's Action
List the workset types you would expect in a simple MEP coordination model.
