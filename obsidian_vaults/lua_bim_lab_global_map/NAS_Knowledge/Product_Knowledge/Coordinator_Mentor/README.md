# Coordinator Mentor Product Knowledge

## Purpose

Prepare BIM coordination mentoring by collecting reviewed coordination patterns, clash triage methods, and model QA workflows.

## Collect

- Coordination decision patterns
- Clash classification examples
- Model QA checklist improvements
- Meeting note structures
- Issue tracking workflows

## Reuse

- Coordination playbook
- Clash triage checklist
- QA checklist
- Reporting template

## Boundary

Do not store or reuse real project names, drawings, models, or clash reports unless explicitly anonymized and reviewed.
