# Starter Plan - 30 Day Launch Curriculum

## Product Promise

Daily productized MEP BIM education for beginners and early-stage BIM learners.

Each lesson delivers:

- one focused MEP BIM topic
- one practical project lens
- three BIM checks
- one common mistake
- one action item
- one scope reminder
