## Practical Case

A duct route may look correct in isolation, but it can fail when ceiling height, lighting, sprinkler, cable tray, and access panel requirements are reviewed together.

## Checklist

- Confirm system intent before modeling
- Review ceiling space by zone
- Track owner discipline for each issue
