## Practical Case

In a real project, strainer and air vent placement can look acceptable in a model view, but still create coordination risk when installation sequence, access, clearance, and model data are reviewed together.

## Checklist

- Confirm supply and return separation
- Review valve and flange access
- Check pipe data against equipment schedules
