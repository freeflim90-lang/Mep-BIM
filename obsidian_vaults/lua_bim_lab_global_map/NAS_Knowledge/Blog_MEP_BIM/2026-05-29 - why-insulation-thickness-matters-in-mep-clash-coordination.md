## Practical Case

A chilled water pipe without modeled insulation may pass a visual check, but the installed outside diameter can conflict with nearby trays or ceiling frames.

## Checklist

- Check pipe outside diameter with insulation
- Separate hard clashes from clearance issues
- Record insulation assumptions in the model QA note
