## Practical Case

In a real project, VAV box access can look acceptable in a model view, but still create coordination risk when installation sequence, access, clearance, and model data are reviewed together.

## Checklist

- Check duct height and insulation
- Confirm access panels before approval
- Compare model routes with mechanical intent
