Day 23 - Mechanical Room Coordination Basics

1. Why This Matters
Mechanical rooms concentrate equipment, pipes, ducts, valves, drains, electrical panels, and maintenance access in one tight area.

2. Core Concept
Mechanical room BIM coordination should review equipment clearance, pipe connection, duct connection, valve access, floor drain logic, and replacement path.

3. Real Project Lens
A pump may fit in the model but still have poor access if valves, strainers, cable trays, or walls block maintenance space.

4. BIM Check
- Check equipment clearance and replacement path.
- Review pipe and duct connection direction.
- Confirm valve, strainer, and drain access.

5. Common Mistake
Beginners often coordinate equipment footprint only and ignore maintenance and replacement space.

6. Today's Action
Choose one mechanical equipment item and list the access zones needed around it.

Scope note:
This is general educational content, not project review, engineering verification, code compliance confirmation, or construction approval.
