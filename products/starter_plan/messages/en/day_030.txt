LUA BIM LABS Starter
Day 30 - Your First Month Starter Review

1. Why This Matters
The first month should give you a practical foundation: systems, drawings, model data, QA, clash thinking, access, installability, and reporting.

2. Core Concept
Starter Plan is not about memorizing every BIM command. It is about building a daily habit of checking model meaning, coordination risk, and practical next actions.

3. Real Project Lens
A beginner who can explain why an issue matters, what to check, and what action to take is already becoming more useful in a BIM team.

4. BIM Check
- Review your strongest topic from the month.
- Identify your weakest topic from the month.
- Choose one habit to repeat for the next 30 days.

5. Common Mistake
Beginners often finish lessons passively. The value comes from turning them into a repeatable checking habit.

6. Today's Action
Write your own 30-day MEP BIM improvement plan with three focus areas: tool skill, system understanding, and coordination judgment.

Scope note:
This is general educational content, not project review, engineering verification, code compliance confirmation, or construction approval.
