Day 6 - Supply Air, Return Air, Exhaust Air, and Outdoor Air

1. Why This Matters
Air systems drive ceiling coordination, equipment connection, fire damper placement, and balancing discussions. Misunderstanding the air system can lead to wrong routes and wrong clash priorities.

2. Core Concept
SA, RA, EA, and OA represent different air purposes. Each system has different routing logic, pressure relationships, equipment connections, and coordination risks.

3. Real Project Lens
A return air duct near a supply air duct may look similar in the model, but its connection, insulation, grille relationship, and balancing intent can be different.

4. BIM Check
- Confirm the system abbreviation in the drawing legend.
- Check whether duct route and terminal connection match the system.
- Review whether color filters clearly separate air systems.

5. Common Mistake
Beginners often model ducts by shape and size only, without understanding what the air system is doing.

6. Today's Action
List four air systems and write one coordination concern for each.
