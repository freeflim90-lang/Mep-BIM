Day 22 - Maintenance Access in MEP BIM

1. Why This Matters
MEP systems must be maintained after construction. BIM coordination should protect access to valves, filters, panels, dampers, and equipment.

2. Core Concept
Maintenance access is the space needed for inspection, operation, replacement, and safe work after installation.

3. Real Project Lens
A valve above a ceiling may not clash with anything, but if no one can reach or operate it, the coordination is weak.

4. BIM Check
- Identify elements that need future access.
- Check access panel or ceiling opening logic.
- Review whether another system blocks operation or replacement.

5. Common Mistake
Beginners often coordinate only physical overlap and miss access needs.

6. Today's Action
Make a list of five MEP elements that usually require maintenance access.

Scope note:
This is general educational content, not project review, engineering verification, code compliance confirmation, or construction approval.
